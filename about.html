FAQs

WHAT IS AN NFT?

NFT stands for “non-fungible token,” a one-of-a-kind digital asset. More info.

WHAT IS CRYPTOCURRENCY?

NFTs are non-fungible digital assets; cryptocurrency is a fungible digital asset (i.e. 1 Bitcoin is the same as any other Bitcoin) or simply, digital money. More info.

WHAT IS A BLOCKCHAIN?

Cryptocurrency & NFTs are digital money & assets, a blockchain is the permanent database of every single transaction that has ever occurred using these digital assets (i.e. buying, selling, trading, etc…). More info.

WHAT IS SOLANA?

We chose to use Solana because it is the fastest, most active & user-friendly blockchain for NFTs, it is also the most environmentally-friendly. More info.
